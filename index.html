Our Mission.

Starting out in this industry is hard, mainly due to the importance of networking to land a job. We believe independent creators have a lot to offer, and talent is getting lost in this competitive industry. Through this platform, we aim to create a place for independent media creators to network, learn from each other, and ultimately, collaborate in projects.

Inclusive

No matter if you have years of experience, or are just starting out, there are projects out there looking for you.

International

Crew media craft can be done remotely, and we believe diversified projects formed by worldwide people can lead to a new era of media projects.
